Canadian Car Consumption

An analysis tool for the fuel consumption statistics of Canadian cars. Find out how fuel efficient your car is, and how it affects the environment.

Get Started

What is this website?

Statistics Canada releases Canadian Fuel Consumption Ratings every year, under the Open Government Licence - Canada . This website allows you to search for any Canadian car between the years of 1995-2015, and obtain federal regulated fuel consumptions stats. Forget the hassle of paying for car consumer reports to get this information!
